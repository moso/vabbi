THE WARCLAW
In order to unlock the mount you need to complete a set of achievements. To unlock the meta achievement you first need to spend 1 rank point in the Warclaw Mastery line. To unlock every Warclaw ability you will need to invest a total ammount of 166 rank points.
  • 1 Rank Point: Unlock the Warclaw companion achievement.
  • 15 Rank Points: While mounted, periodically grant nearby allies the ability to run at warclaw speed.
  • 20 Rank Points: Your warclaw gains an extra stamina bar.
  • 25 Rank Points: Battle Maul now finishes enemies who are in a downed state.
  • 30 Rank Points: Unlock a second skill that allow your warclaw to detect enemies.
  • 35 Rank Points: Unlock a third skill that allows your warclaw to pull on gates to damage them.
  • 40 Rank Points: Unlock a fourth skill that enables you to throw a spear at a mounted player, dismounting you both.
WARCLAW COMPANION META ACHIEVEMENT
The merchant to purchase some of the Warclaw armor pieces is Elvie the Warclaw Tender. One of the achievement is not possible to be achieved in Edge of the Mists.
The achievement is located in the competitive section "World vs World Warclaw"

BUY THE WARCLAW
After you finished the collection, you need to purchase the mount by going back to the spawn of one WvW Map and buy the mount for 8 gold.

THE GUILDRIDER WARCLAW SKIN
The Guildrider Warclaw is the first ever obtainable in game mount skin released. When representing a guild, the Warclaw will display the guild emblem on its equipped armor. To unlock the reward track you have to purchase it for 200 gold from Cynara.
